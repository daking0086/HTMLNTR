================================================================================
CHARACTER FILE: RICHARD HAYES
================================================================================
Role:        Husband / Player proxy
Age:         Mid 30s
Partner:     Ayesha Khan (wife)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Richard is a self-made American millionaire — disciplined, controlling, and used
to solving problems with money and influence. Detained in a foreign airport, he
is locked in an empty office with only a secret phone call connecting him to
Ayesha. He becomes a helpless witness to what happens to her.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CORE TRAITS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Control-oriented — built his empire through discipline and rules
• Protective and possessive of Ayesha
• Proud of self-made success; expects the world to respect that
• Powerless when local authorities ignore his status
• Emotionally restrained until fear and rage break through
• Carries guilt for leaving her alone (even if not his choice)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PERSONALITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Calm, direct, problem-solver mindset
• Tries to guide Ayesha through the phone when he cannot be there
• Frustration turns to panic when he watches but cannot intervene
• Player choices reflect his moral line: comfort vs. obsession vs. control
• Not a cartoon villain — a man losing the one thing he controlled most

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BACKGROUND
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Built a large import-export business from nothing in the USA
• Traveling abroad for business when detained without clear timeline
• Held in a bare detention office — no lawyer, no leverage
• Secretly uses video call to see Ayesha and assess danger

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SPEECH STYLE (FOR WRITERS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Low, careful voice when hiding the call from guards
• Pet names: "baby," "Ayesha," "jaan" (less often — more Ayesha's word to him)
• Crisis: short sentences, repetition, helpless commands ("Answer me!")
• Rarely monologues — reacts to what he sees on the feed

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RELATIONSHIPS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ayesha      — Wife he loves; his weakness and motivation
Authorities — Faceless power that negates his wealth
Player      — Richard's choices ARE the player's moral decisions

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STORY / GAME ROLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Player avatar in choice scenes
• Helpless witness in linear phone/video scenes
• Affection rises: protective, reassuring, trusting responses
• Corruption rises: voyeuristic, demanding, cold, controlling responses
• Stats track how Ayesha trusts him vs. how far she may drift

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WRITING RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Competent in America, powerless abroad — show the contrast
2. Give him reactions during video scenes — he is not silent wallpaper
3. His detention caused the situation; he carries that weight
4. Do not make him magically escape to save her in Release 1
5. Break his composure gradually, not all at once
