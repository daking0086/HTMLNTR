================================================================================
CHARACTER FILE: AYESHA KHAN
================================================================================
Role:        Wife / Main heroine
Age:         Late 20s
Partner:     Richard Hayes (husband)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ayesha is Richard Hayes' wife — raised in American luxury, modest in how she
dresses, but arrogant in how she sees the world. She expects status, money, and
her husband's name to protect her. This trip abroad is the first time reality
refuses to cooperate.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CORE TRAITS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Slightly arrogant and biased toward people she sees as "below" her
• Sheltered — no real experience with danger, corruption, or poverty
• Loyal to Richard, but blames him when she feels abandoned or afraid
• Performs confidence to hide fear (especially when guards might be watching)
• Humiliation hits her PRIDE before anything else
• Emotionally cracks hard once arrogance stops working

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PERSONALITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Entitled and impatient when uncomfortable
• Name-drops Richard's business and lawyers as weapons
• Disgusted by crude men, foreign airports, and being treated "like locals"
• Believes threats about Richard's release because she has no street sense
• Ashamed afterward — hates that she was naive, not just that she was hurt
• Becomes clingy on the phone once the night turns violent

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BACKGROUND
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Grew up in the USA: private schools, gated communities, hired help
• Married a self-made millionaire and lived inside a protected bubble
• Dresses modestly (hijab, long sleeves, flowing skirt) but carries elite posture
• Never learned what happens when no one cares who her husband is

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SPEECH STYLE (FOR WRITERS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Default: sharp, annoyed, superior
• Insults: "low-class," "animals," "beneath me," "backward"
• Vulnerable: "jaan," whispers, voice breaking, shorter sentences
• On hidden call: quiet, controlled, trying not to alert guards
• After trauma: bitter, self-blaming, still needs Richard on the line

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RELATIONSHIPS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Richard     — Love + dependence + blame when he cannot save her
Guards      — Men who strip away her illusion of safety
Workers     — "People beneath her" she may still need for rescue

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STORY / GAME ROLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Main emotional POV the player watches through Richard's phone
• Affection: trust, comfort, protection
• Corruption: shame, curiosity, dependence on strangers, broken pride
• Gallery: moments Richard sees or she survives

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WRITING RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start arrogant — end shaken (not the reverse)
2. She is NOT stupid; she is UNPREPARED
3. Confidence is performance, not bravery
4. Threats about Richard WORK on her because she is sheltered
5. Recovery is partial — she does not bounce back in one scene
